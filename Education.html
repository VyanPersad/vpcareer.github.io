MSC. Petroleum Engineering

The University of The West Indies

2017

The MSc. was undertaken at a time when I was employed within the oil and gas sector. Having grown up in an oil (and gas) town (Fyzabad) and finding a job within the sector it was a some what personal endeavour to try and learn as much and hopefully apply it as quickly as possible

Having grown up within walking distance of production stations, pumping jacks drilling and workover rigs and the then prevailing job and economic situation it seemed te best option for both advancement and a certain technical challenge.

My MSc. in Petroleum Engineering has led me to develop mobile software that enables the user to perform a multitude of calculations pertaining to the oil and gas field. This work has given me tremendous insight into the new technological tools that are available to young engineers and the knowledge on how best to utilize them, especially within the oil and gas industry. This course gave the student the insight into, and practice with real world scenarios related to oil and gas field development and production.
